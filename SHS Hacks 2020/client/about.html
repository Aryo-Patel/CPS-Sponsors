About the Creators

The goal of Synccess is to connect people in the community with school groups in need of funding and resources. As students offered the privilege of a robust and diverse extracurricular system, we are well aware of the value of reliable access to resources. Our website could be instrumental in building relationships between mentors/sponsors and students, providing better resources to school organizations in need, and inspiring students to continue to grow and explore. Even a small boost can help to boost our education system by building intrinsic motivation for learning.
